About Us

Purple Label Studio

Based in Los Angeles, Purple Label Studio specializes in premium custom embroidery, screen printing, and apparel decoration. We work with brands, businesses, teams, and individuals to create high-quality custom products.

From intricate chain stitch work to bold 3D puff embroidery, our team brings decades of experience and a passion for craftsmanship to every project. Whether you need 12 pieces or 12,000 — we've got you covered.
